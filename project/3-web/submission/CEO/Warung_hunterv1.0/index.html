Nasi Lemak Antarabangsa

Nasi Lemak Antarabangsa is one of the popular and oldest nasi lemak brands in Kuala Lumpur, which has been around since the 1970s. It was created by Ms. Normi and has received extensive coverage on local television programs. In Kampung Baru, Kuala Lumpur, the Nasi Lemak Antarabangsa stall is always crowded, especially at night, as it serves as a gathering place. The nasi lemak provided can be eaten with a variety of side dishes such as cockles, squid sambal, lungs, rendang, and more. The peak hours, especially on weekends, can be quite challenging to get a packet of nasi lemak, but it's worth it.

Warung Hijau

This eatery located in Kampung Sungai Penchala, KL is highly recommended if you're craving authentic village flavors with a wide selection of delicious dishes. Available from lunchtime to dinner, this restaurant never fails to attract local customers. They serve various traditional village dishes such as smoked meat cooked in spicy coconut milk, creamy prawns, crab curry, assorted salads, raw vegetables, and much more. If we count the variety of dishes available, it easily exceeds 15 types. Although Warung Sambal Hijau operates from early morning until late at night every day, the best time to come and try their extensive and still piping hot menu is around 9 am to 12 noon. The dishes here sell out quickly, so make sure to come early.

Warung Satay Zaini

When it comes to satay, the first place that comes to mind is Warung Satay Hj. Samuri in Kajang, which is a popular dining spot for tourists in Selangor. However, if you're in Kuala Lumpur and looking for a delicious alternative in satay flavors, why not visit Satay Zaini on Jalan Kolam Ayer Lama in Ampang Jaya, Kuala Lumpur? Satay Zaini, which has been established for over 35 years in KL, can be considered the closest rival to Satay Kajang. They offer various types of satay, such as chicken, beef, lamb, young intestine, chicken liver, and spleen. The most popular choices here are the beef satay and young intestine satay. Not to be forgotten is their rich and flavorful peanut sauce, which is unique and complements the satay at Satay Zaini. It's best to come early to this place, as they tend to run out of food if you arrive late.

Satay Capitol 1951 – Warisan

For satay enthusiasts, there is another destination that you shouldn't miss to experience the deliciousness of their satay. The beef satay is incredibly tender and has a more flavorful spice blend compared to most other satay stalls. The peanut sauce is also tasty and not overly spicy. In addition to satay, they also sell fried food and soto, which are equally popular here. There are two branches available in Kuala Lumpur, one in Pusat Penjaja Selera Muda, Kampung Baru, and the other in Capital Cafe, Jalan TAR. You can visit whichever branch is closest to you.

Nasi Ayam Hainan Taman Melawati

The menu is simple, and the location of the restaurant is quite ordinary, but the reception during lunchtime is exceptionally crowded. This eatery is located in Medan Selera Taman Melawati and managed by a Chinese convert couple who have been established in KL for over 30 years. It offers affordable and delicious Hainanese chicken rice for those who want to personally experience its scrumptiousness. The restaurant operates every day except Thursdays, starting from 11 am until the food runs out. Typically, everything is sold out before Asr prayer time.

Mak Teh Ikan Bakar Pasar Dato’ Keramat

Mak Teh Ikan Bakar Pasar Dato' Keramat specializes in preparing fresh fish and grilling it to perfection. They offer a variety of fish options, such as stingray, snapper, tilapia, and more. The fish is marinated in a flavorful blend of spices and then grilled over charcoal, resulting in a smoky and aromatic dish.

We Are Warung Hunter

Warung Hunter is a renowned food establishment that has earned a reputation as the best warung-style eatery in Malaysia. With a rich culinary heritage and a commitment to quality, Warung Hunter has become a must-visit destination for both locals and tourists seeking an authentic Malaysian dining experience.At Warung Hunter, the focus is on serving delectable traditional dishes that are true to the essence of Malaysian cuisine.
